# Northbridge Digital Workforce Inventory v1.0

This document is the canonical **Northbridge Digital Workforce Inventory v1.0**.

## Guiding principle

> **Specialists are reusable. Teams are products.**

A specialist is never built for a single industry. Teams assemble specialists with industry knowledge and workflows.

## Future expansion

This inventory is intentionally modular.

```
Specialists
        ↓
Functional Teams
        ↓
Industry Teams
        ↓
Managers (future)
        ↓
Directors (future)
        ↓
Vice Presidents (future)
```

This aligns with the NEO philosophy:

- **Specialists** become reusable platform capabilities.
- **Functional teams** become standardized Northbridge Digital products.
- **Industry teams** are composed from those reusable building blocks with industry-specific knowledge layered on top, allowing rapid expansion into new verticals without reinventing the workforce each time.
