# Northbridge Venture Group – Launch Checklist

## Branding

- Confirm final logo and wordmark are in place.
- Verify color palette (black, white, red `#B11226`) is consistent across all pages.
- Check typography and spacing look consistent and professional on desktop and mobile.

## Metadata

- Confirm site title and description in `app/layout.tsx`.
- Verify Open Graph image (`/og-image.png`) exists and loads correctly.
- Check page titles and descriptions for:
  - Home
  - About
  - Ventures
  - Services
  - Partner With Us
  - Clients
  - Contact

## Portfolio Links

- Confirm **Aviator Network** links to `https://aviatornetwork.com`.
- Confirm **AirTax Financial** links to `https://airtaxfinancial.com`.
- Ensure **Royal International Flight School** appears only as a **client**, not a venture.

## Contact Information

- Verify `contact@northbridgeventuregroup.com` is visible and clickable.
- Confirm any additional emails are correct.
- Test contact form submission (if implemented).

## Domain Readiness

- Confirm domain is connected in Vercel.
- Verify DNS records are correctly configured.
- Ensure HTTPS is active.
- Check root and `www` redirect properly.

## Placeholder Assets to Replace Later

- Replace temporary `favicon.ico` with branded favicon.
- Replace `/og-image.png` with final social preview image.
- Remove any placeholder text such as "coming soon".
